# WEB-24 Wordmark Review

Recommended direction: `humanist-sans`

The three concept families live in [docs/wordmark-concepts](/Users/sunny/code/riddim-website-web-24/docs/wordmark-concepts) with PNG exports at `64px` and `256px` height for founder review.

1. `humanist-sans`
   Font: Overpass Semibold (permissive open-source family)
   Why it works: the counters stay open at small sizes, the rhythm feels calm instead of playful, and the letterforms fit the existing product-company positioning without drifting into game branding.
2. `contemporary-serif`
   Font: Libre Baskerville Bold (permissive open-source family)
   Why it was not chosen: it brings useful gravitas, but the serif detailing gets soft sooner at the smaller review size and feels a little more editorial than product-focused.
3. `geometric-grotesque`
   Font: Cooper Hewitt Bold (permissive open-source family)
   Why it was not chosen: it is clear and compact, but the geometry reads more industrial and less warm than the recommended direction.

Final committed assets:

- `wordmark.svg`: Overpass Semibold outline, optimized for header use on dark backgrounds
- `monogram.svg`: companion `R` glyph for future favicon/social workflows

Legibility check:

- Reviewed locally at `24px` and `96px` heights against a dark background before wiring into the site build.
