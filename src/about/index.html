About

Riddim is a small product company with one person behind it.

I'm Sunny Purewal, the founder of Riddim Software, a one-person indie product company in Hamilton, ON, Canada.

Riddim builds apps, web tools, and small things that are useful enough to ship. The work usually starts with a practical itch, gets pared down to the simplest version that can help someone, and then improves in public one careful release at a time.
